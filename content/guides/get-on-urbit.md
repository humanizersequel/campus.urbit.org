When it comes down to it, Urbit is a *personal server*, and servers are meant to run 24/7.
It's possible to [run a ship locally](/guides/get-on-urbit?id=local-planet), but not ideal.
One of the greatest conceits of Urbit is that you can reap all the benefits of cloud computing,
but instead of a murky relationship between your data and a cloud provider (and their cloud provider),
they just run a computer for you.
